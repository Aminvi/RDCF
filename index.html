RDCF was created to replace fedral - State Adult Assistance Programs that served the same purpose, but were administered by the State Agencies and recieved criticism for lacking consistent eligibility criteria- The restructuring of this programs was intended to Standardize the eligibility requirements and level of benefits
RDCF is funded from the U.S Treasury general funds, not the Social security trust fund. As of December 2023, The program provided benefits to approximately two million americans.
